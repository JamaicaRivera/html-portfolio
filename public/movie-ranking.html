The Best Movies According to Jamaica Rivera

My top 3 movies of all-time

Dayo

It is a FIlipino animated movie. I like it a lot. It reminds me of my childhood which is the best part of it.

The Perks of Being a Wallflower

This is one of my favorite movie. The protagonist is a loner and unable to speak his heart.

The Best of Me

I love the actors in this movie. The settings is very beautiful. I love how the story turns around all the time.
